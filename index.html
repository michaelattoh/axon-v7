INTEGRATED TAX ADMINISTRATION SYSTEM (ITAS)

Streamline your taxes today

  • Fully Integrated Tax Administration System: Our systemmakes tax transactions easy for the taxpayers and tax administration easy for the tax administrators.
  • Electronic Filing and Payment: The system enables taxpayers to file their tax returns and make payments online, eliminating the need for manual submission of tax returns and payments at tax offices.
  • Compliance Monitoring: The taxt management system is able to monitor taxpayer compliance and send reminders to those who have nor filled their tax returns or paid their tax returns.

HEALTH PRO

Healthcare Service

  • Electronic Medical Records (EMR): A centralized, digital repository for patient health information, including medical history, medications, allergies, and laboratory results.
  • Appointment Scheduling and Reminders: An intuitive appointment scheduling system that enables patients to book appointments online, while also sending automated reminders.
  • Billing and Insurance Management: Integrated billing module simplifies the process of insurance claims, invoicing, and payment collection, ensuring accurate and timely reimbursement.

BIOMETRIC VOTER MANAGEMENT

E Registers

  • Biometric Data Collection: The BVMS is able to collect biometric data such as fingerprints, facial recognition, or any other biometric data as required by the election commission.
  • Real-time Authentication: The BVMS performs authentication of voters in real-time during the voting process, to ensure that only eligible voters are allowed to cast their votes.
  • Offline Functionality: The BVMS is capabile of operating offline in case of network connectivity issues or power outages.
